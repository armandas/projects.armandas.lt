About me
--------

I am a recent graduate with Masters in Computer Systems Engineering from the
`University of Sussex <http://www.sussex.ac.uk>`_. I am currently working as an
electronics design engineer. My main area of interest is digital electronics,
such as FPGAs and embedded systems. During the third year at university, I did
an individual project titled :ref:`third-year-project`. For our MEng group
project we were working on :ref:`meng-group-project`.
