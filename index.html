About me¶

I am a recent graduate with Masters in Computer Systems Engineering from the University of Sussex. I am currently working as an electronics design engineer. My main area of interest is digital electronics, such as FPGAs and embedded systems. During the third year at university, I did an individual project titled FPGA Based VGA Driver and Arcade Game. For our MEng group project we were working on Small-scale Biofuel Production.

Feel free to contact me by email: jarusauskas@gmail.com
