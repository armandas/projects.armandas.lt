Handheld DSO Comparison Table¶

date:7 April, 2013

I wanted to get a better view of the mid-range handheld DSO options available in the market and decided to make a comparison table. I chose not to include toy DSOs, like DSO Nano, or expensive, big-brand scopes. You can download the documents below.
